1. Choose the sentence with the correct use of the subjunctive mood:
a) If I was you, I would take the job.
b) If I were you, I would take the job.
c) If I am you, I would take the job.
d) If I be you, I would take the job.

Answer: b) If I were you, I would take the job.

2. Identify the sentence with the correct use of the hyphen:
a) He is a well known writer.
b) He is a well-known writer.
c) He is a wellknown writer.
d) He is a wellknown-writer.

Answer: b) He is a well-known writer.

3. What is the correct form of the irregular verb "swim" in the past perfect tense?
a) Swim
b) Swam
c) Swum
d) Swimming

Answer: c) Swum

4. Choose the sentence with the correct use of the gerund:
a) She enjoys to swim in the ocean.
b) She enjoys swimming in the ocean.
c) She enjoys swim in the ocean.
d) She enjoys swam in the ocean.

Answer: b) She enjoys swimming in the ocean.

5. Identify the sentence with the correct use of the relative pronoun:
a) The girl who I met her yesterday is my friend.
b) The girl whom I met yesterday is my friend.
c) The girl whose I met yesterday is my friend.
d) The girl which I met yesterday is my friend.

Answer: b) The girl whom I met yesterday is my friend.
